Our Story

Founded in 2018, Green Leaf Cafe was born from a simple belief: that what we consume should nourish both our bodies and our environment.

We partner with local organic farms and fair-trade suppliers to bring you the freshest, most sustainable ingredients. Every cup of coffee, every bite of pastry tells a story of conscious choices and mindful living.
